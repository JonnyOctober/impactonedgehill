A developer is seeking a variance to add a third story to an already non-conforming building located at 172-174 Main Street (Block 89, Lot 6). The building is currently set back less than 10 feet from our property at 10 Edgehill, whereas zoning regulations require a 30-foot setback.

If approved, this variance could:

  • Increase the building height by 12 feet, which may affect sunlight exposure for adjacent properties.
  • Introduce additional windows facing neighboring yards, potentially impacting privacy.
  • Expand a non-conforming structure, which may set a precedent for future variance requests in the area.

We encourage the Planning Board to carefully evaluate this proposal and its potential effects on privacy, sunlight, and neighborhood character.
